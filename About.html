About Us

Who are we

Point of connection for Trading consists of its headquarters in Riyadh with its multiple departments such as sales, marketing, clearance,warehouses and logistical support consisting of a number of distribution cars that enable us to serve the network of our customers, partners and agents spread in the most important governorates in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

History

Point of connection for Trading was established in 2013 and is a Saudi Facility working in the field of importing and distribution and marketing snack foodstuffs and ready-made clothes and its head office is in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, it is one of the branches of the Ideal Business Trading and Contracting Company Group and its commercial arm 2013.

Visions

Our vision is to become the leading provider of distribution and marketing solutions, utilizing the latest technology and data-driven insights to ensure our customers are always ahead of the competition. We will strive to develop innovative strategies that target the right customers and create meaningful relationships. We will also focus on providing high-quality services and products that meet the needs of our customers and drive their success. We will continually invest in our technology and data capabilities to stay ahead of the curve. Our goal is to become the go-to source for distribution and marketing solutions and become the trusted partner for our customers

Company Value

Our Company is concerned with the core values of achievement and success in its field. These values include hard work, creativity, sincerity and responsibility. Customers and employees are jointly respected in accordance with the established rules. It has a completely reliable system of efficiency and performance that ensures maximum achievement at every stage. Other views are respected and changes are properly and effectively answered. The application of the applicable laws and regulations issued by the Authority is of great importance to it. In addition to coordinating with others who work in the same field

Our goals

The main goals of Point of connection are to provide integrated distribution services by providing the following

1

Enhance the company's brand image by providing excellent distribution services and strengthening relationships with customers, suppliers and other stakeholders

2

Provide fast and integrated distribution of all products and create an excellent customer experience by improving the fast and accurate distribution of products

3

Ensure the safety and accuracy of the products being distributed and improve the cost of distribution by reducing wastage and utilizing efficient distribution methods

4

Improve the organizational efficiency of distribution by obtaining accurate and reliable information and improve the level of service provided by distribution by providing clear communication, on-time delivery and real-time tracking information

5

Increase customer satisfaction by providing quality and timely services
